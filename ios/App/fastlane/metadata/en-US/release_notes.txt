- Past Lessons redesigned: tap a lesson to expand it in place — no more modal popping over the list. Smooth open/close animation, multiple ✓ marks for replays.
- Daily audio + Read tabs now support horizontal swipe between sub-tabs (Today ↔ Past Lessons; Discover ↔ My Articles ↔ Classroom ↔ Search).
- Read tabs reorganized: Discover, My Articles, Classroom, and a Search icon on the right. Read remembers the last tab you visited.
- Discover gets a "Topics: …" chip at the top showing what's curating your feed, with a tap-to-customize gear.

- Once you simplify an article, the original disappears from Discover and My Articles, and the simplified version is what opens when you tap it.
- Pull-to-refresh on Discover and My Articles now actually fetches fresh data. Leaving the reader also refreshes the feed automatically.
- Search now full-width with a cleaner saved-searches page; closing a search returns to the search home; results page no longer reloads the whole app.
